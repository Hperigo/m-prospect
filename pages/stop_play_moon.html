Stop Play Moon

An artistic project for the Brazilian band Stop Play Moon, this video production features geometric shapes that move, adapt and vibrate according to the rhythm of the musical sounds being played.

Animated with After Effects and Blender, this visual content was art directed and executed by me. In partnership with film director Rodrigo Beloto, I built visuals that slowly shift from perspective angles to a twisted shape.

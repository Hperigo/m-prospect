White Mirror

The White Mirror celebrates people’s ever present auras. The sculpture tracks movement through webcams and OpenCV. When two people cross each other, their auras "collide", creating an explosion of colour.

Technical overview

To develop White Mirror, I was in charge of developing the OpenCV Tracking system. I worked alongside Dimitre Lima (dmtr.org) and the product designers of Spaces and Creatures.

Because the installation received varying natural lighting throughout the day, we had to produce an intelligent system that divides the camera feed into several sub-tiles. If a specific tile doesn’t have a movement on it, its image would be used as the mask for background subtraction. This allowed us to generate tracking blobs that worked in the morning, noon and night.

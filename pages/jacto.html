Jacto

Process Reel

Jacto, a heavy agricultural machine manufacturer here in Brazil, was founded by a Japanese immigrant family. Futurebrand, a branding agency, was tasked to bring the company's traditional aspects back to its Branding guidelines. Kirigami, a conventional art technique similar to Origami, was used as inspiration to the graphical elements.

I was commissioned to develop software that generates complex shapes using five iconic figures of Kirigami. The initial idea was to deliver a simple vector editing tool that creates the iconography using a Bezier curve to guide the icons' position and rotation. The final result became something similar to Adobe Illustrator, with full Bezier editing (add, delete, subdivide), brushes, ctrl+z, custom file saving, PNG/SVG exports—all done in Javascript and Paper.js.
